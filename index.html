How It Works

  • Step 1

    Clean Face Before Applying DNA Complex

    Wash face thoroughly with cleanser, rinse, and pat dry. PLEASE THOROUGHLY ALLOW skin to dry before applying DNA Complex.

  • Step 2

    Apply DNA Complex

    Shake bottle thoroughly. Use one-half (1/2) pump for targeted areas such as around the eyes. Use two (2) pumps to cover your entire face and neck. After skin has thoroughly dried, you can apply the rest of your skincare products in your normal sequence.

  • Step 3

    Continue to Use

    Continue to use DNA Complex twice a day (morning and bedtime) for ongoing results. Many users report a lasting effect that results in tighter, smoother skin with continuous use.
